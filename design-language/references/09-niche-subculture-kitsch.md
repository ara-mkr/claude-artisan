# Niche Subculture & Kitsch Aesthetics

*41 styles in this family (1 flagship). Generated from `scripts/style_catalog.json` — the single source of truth.*

Internet-native and subcultural moods — dark academia, cottagecore, webcore, weirdcore, coquette, brat, quiet-luxury and dozens more. **These are mood/flavor references, not structural UI systems.** They tell you palette, type feeling, imagery, and vibe — but you still need a real layout/component system underneath.

> **How to apply this family.** **Use these as an accent layer over a solid structural style.** Pull the palette, type mood, and motifs, then apply them on top of (say) a minimalist or editorial layout. Don't try to build a whole design system out of a TikTok aesthetic alone.

### Dark Academia  ⭐ *flagship — full spec in [00-flagship-implementation-specs/dark-academia.md](00-flagship-implementation-specs/dark-academia.md)*

*Slug:* `dark-academia` · *Era:* 2015–present (peak 2020)

**Origin.** Tumblr/TikTok subculture romanticizing classic scholarship.

**Defining traits.**
- Moody browns, oxblood, forest green, cream
