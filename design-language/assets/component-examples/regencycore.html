The Featherington Post Season Almanac

Dearest reader,

A slim gold hairline sits inset within a soft blush frame, like the edge of a miniature portrait — it repeats around the nav, every card, and the invitation form below.

Diamond of the Season

A gilt frame

Every raised surface wears the same double border, like a locket.

Pastel and pearl

Blush, gold, and sage stand in for the season's ribbons and roses.
